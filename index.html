Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock




Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock, is a game popularized by TV show "Big Bang Theory" it is an expansion on the popular hand motion game Rock-Paper-Scissors, Where each choice you make is defeated by 2 of the other options and beats the 2 of the other options instead of each choice beating 1 thing and being defeated by the other

To play the game here, you will need to refresh this page and enter your choice of Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock when promted, you will play against a computer who will pick Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock at random and then you will be promted if you were: victorious, lost or tied against the computer
